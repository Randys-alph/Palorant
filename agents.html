Reyna

Duelist

Forged in the heart of Mexico, Reyna dominates single combat, popping off with each kill she scores. Her capability is only limited by her raw skill, making her highly dependent on performance.

Abilities

Q
Devour
Enemies killed by Reyna leave behind Soul Orbs that last 3 seconds. Consume a nearby Soul Orb to rapidly heal for a short duration. Health gained through this skill exceeding 100 will decay over time.
E
Dismiss
Consume a nearby Soul Orb to become intangible for a short duration. If Empress is active, also become invisible.
C
Leer
Cast an ethereal, destructible eye a short distance forward. The eye will nearsight all enemies who look at it.
X
Empress
Enter a frenzy, increasing firing, equip and reload speed dramatically. Scoring a kill renews the duration. Enemies killed during this ability leave behind Soul Orbs that last twice as long as normal.
